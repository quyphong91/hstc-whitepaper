01 Introduction

What Is HSTC?

HSTC (tracuuhs.com) is a free online tool that helps Vietnamese businesses, customs brokers, and importers/exporters find the correct HS Code for any product — quickly and accurately.

💡 What is an HS Code?

Every product traded internationally has a unique number called an HS Code (Harmonized System Code). Think of it like a product's "tax ID" — it determines how much import duty you pay, whether your goods can be imported, and what paperwork is needed. Getting the wrong code is a costly mistake. HSTC helps you find the right one.

Who Uses This Website?

Importers & Exporters

Businesses that need to declare goods at Vietnamese customs. Finding the right HS code upfront avoids fines and shipment delays.

🧾

Professionals who file customs declarations on behalf of businesses. They use HSTC to quickly verify classifications.

People learning customs classification or researching trade policy who need to understand the HS structure.

02 What It Can Do

Key Features

HSTC offers six main tools, each designed to help users at different stages of the classification process.

🔍

Keyword Search

Type a product name or description in Vietnamese or English. The system searches through all official HS descriptions and legal notes to find matches. Works even without perfect spelling — diacritics are handled automatically.

🧠

Smart (Semantic) Search

Goes beyond keywords — understands the meaning of what you type. For example, searching "protective headwear for construction" will find "safety helmets" even if those exact words aren't in your query.

🤖

AI Classification

Describe your product in detail and the AI will analyse it step-by-step, applying the official GRI classification rules, and suggest the most likely HS code — with its reasoning clearly explained.

📚

Reference Library

Full access to official HS Explanatory Notes (EN), Supplementary Explanatory Notes (SEN), GRI rules 1–6, WCO Classification Compendium, and the full Vietnamese tariff schedule with MFN import duty rates.

⚖️

Official Rulings

A searchable database of real classification decisions issued by Vietnamese Customs (TCHQ notifications). Useful for finding precedents — "how was a similar product classified before?"

📰

News & Guides

Articles explaining classification updates, regulatory changes, and practical guides written for practitioners — not just lawyers.

05 Core Feature

How Keyword Search Works

When you type a word or phrase into the search bar, the website runs it through a smart ranking system to find the most relevant HS codes — not just any codes that happen to contain your word.

💡 Analogy

Think of it like Google Search, but specifically for HS codes. Instead of just looking for exact word matches, it weighs how significant a word is. A rare, specific word like "thermostat" counts for much more than a common word like "device".

How a Search Query is Processed — Step by Step

Why Does It Handle Vietnamese Spelling Well?

Vietnamese uses accent marks (diacritics) that can be typed many different ways. The search engine automatically strips these marks before comparing — so searching "ao choang tam" and "áo choàng tắm" produce identical results. No need to worry about typing accents correctly.

Search Filters Explained

🗂️

Section Filter

HS codes are organised into 21 Sections (e.g., Section XI covers Textiles). Use this to narrow your search to a specific industry or product type.

📂

Chapter Filter

Each Section is divided into Chapters. For example, Chapter 63 covers "Other made-up textile articles." Filter by chapter if you already have a general idea.

📋

Has Notes Filter

Show only results that have matching text from the official Explanatory Notes — giving you legal evidence to support the classification.

🎯

Exact Match

By default the system shows results that match at least 60% of your words. Turn on "Exact Match" to require all your words to be present.

06 AI-Powered Feature

How Smart (Semantic) Search Works

Smart Search understands the meaning of your words, not just the words themselves. It can find the right HS code even when you use different vocabulary than what appears in the official documents.

💡 Analogy

Regular keyword search is like a library assistant who only looks for the exact title you say. Smart Search is like an expert librarian who understands that "running shoes" and "athletic footwear" refer to the same category, and finds the right shelf even if you use different words.

Smart Search — How It Works
sequenceDiagram participant U as You participant W as Website participant AI as AI Service (OpenAI) participant DB as Search Index U->>W: Type: "giày chạy bộ" W->>AI: "Expand this into a richer description\nincluding synonyms and technical terms" AI-->>W: "Giày thể thao, giày chạy bộ,\nrunning shoes, athletic footwear,\nsports shoes, sneakers..." W->>AI: "Convert this expanded description\ninto a mathematical fingerprint (vector)" AI-->>W: A list of 1,536 numbers representing\nthe meaning of the query W->>DB: "Find all HS note chunks whose\nfingerprint is closest to mine" DB-->>W: Top 20 closest matches\nwith similarity scores (0–100%) W->>W: Remove duplicates\nKeep best match per heading code W-->>U: Results ranked by similarity\nShows heading code + matching text\n+ similarity percentage

What Is a "Fingerprint" (Vector)?

Plain English Explanation

Every piece of text can be converted into a list of numbers — 1,536 of them — that captures its meaning. Texts with similar meanings produce similar numbers. The Smart Search system pre-converts all HS notes into these number lists and stores them. When you search, it converts your query into numbers too, then finds the notes whose numbers are most similar. This is why it understands meaning, not just words.

07 AI-Powered Feature

How AI Classification Works

The AI Classification feature is the most powerful tool on the site. You describe your product in detail, and the AI walks through the official classification process step by step — just like an expert customs officer would.

💡 Analogy

Think of it as having a senior customs classification expert available 24/7. You describe your product, and the expert: (1) searches through all relevant HS notes, (2) reads the official legal text carefully, (3) compares the most likely codes, and (4) gives you a recommendation with full reasoning — citing the exact paragraphs that support the decision.

What Does the AI Output Look Like?

1

Step 1 — Initial Shortlist

The AI lists 3–5 candidate HS headings it found through its search, and briefly explains why each one might apply based on the official notes.

2

Step 2 — Narrowing Down Using Legal Notes

The AI quotes the actual legal text from the Explanatory Notes — showing which headings are ruled out and which are confirmed based on the official language.

3

Step 3 — Final Subheading Comparison

Compares the 2–3 remaining candidates at the most detailed level (6 or 8 digits) and selects the best fit, citing which GRI rule was applied.

✓

Final Result

A clear recommendation with the HS code, a confidence rating, a one-paragraph explanation, and links to the official notes on tracuuhs.com so you can verify independently.

⚠️ Important Note About AI Results

The AI classification is a starting point and reference tool, not an official determination. Always verify the result with a qualified customs professional or the General Department of Vietnam Customs before making an official declaration. The AI itself includes this reminder in every response.

Usage Limits

To keep the service sustainable, each user can make 5 AI classification requests per day. The system tracks this by the user's internet address (IP) — anonymised for privacy. There is no login or registration required.

Each Service Explained Simply

🗄️

Supabase — "The Database & Security Guard"

Supabase is the backbone. It stores all the live data (articles, requests, logs), handles admin login securely, and runs the "background tasks" (called Edge Functions) that power AI features. Think of it as the building that houses everything.

🤖

OpenAI — "The AI Brain"

When you use Smart Search or AI Classification, the website calls OpenAI's services. One model (GPT-4o-mini) understands language and generates text. Another (text-embedding-3-small) converts text into the numerical fingerprints used by Smart Search.

🔎

hstc-api — "The Fast Search Engine"

A dedicated search service that the AI pipeline uses to quickly find candidate HS headings. When you run AI Classification, this engine searches through all HS notes in milliseconds to give the AI the best starting candidates.

Why Is the Website Fast?

⚡

Search Is Instant

The entire HS code library is loaded into your browser when you first visit. Keyword searches don't need to contact a server — they run directly on your device in milliseconds.

📦

Only Load What You Need

Pages you haven't visited yet aren't downloaded until you actually click on them. This keeps the initial load fast.

🌍

Edge Computing

AI tasks run on servers located close to the user geographically — reducing the time data needs to travel through the internet.
